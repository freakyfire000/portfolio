Who I am.

My name is Francesco Marchetti, I am 22 years old, and I study Interfaces and Communication Technologies at the University of Trento. Over the years, I have learned to work in teams and to be a fair leader who listens to others' ideas.

In my second year of high school, I participated in the FIRST Lego League Challenge, winning the award for "Best Innovative Project." Outside of school, I have been playing the violin at the Conservatory of Reggio Emilia for 8 years, traveling across Europe to perform with different orchestras.

I am also the president of the GruppoGiovani SGSM association in Novellara, where I coordinate sports and cultural events to improve the quality of life in local districts and foster social interaction. My greatest passion is the world of comics and cinematography.
